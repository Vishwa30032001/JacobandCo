About Us

Jacob & Co. is a luxury watch and jewelry company founded by Jacob Arabo, a jeweler known for his bold and extravagant designs. Established in 1986 in New York City, Jacob & Co. quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to watchmaking and jewelry craftsmanship. Jacob & Co. watches are renowned for pushing the boundaries of traditional watchmaking, with models incorporating complications such as tourbillons, minute repeaters, astronomical displays, and more.
